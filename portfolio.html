BMW M8

Introduced in June 2019, the M8 was initially produced in the 2-door convertible (F91 model code) and 2-door coupe (F92 model code) body styles.[2][3] A 4-door sedan (F93 model code, marketed as 'Gran Coupe') body style was added to the lineup in October 2019. The M8 is powered by the BMW S63 twin-turbocharged V8 engine shared with the BMW M5 (F90).

Beetle


The Volkswagen Beetle is a compact car offered by the German carmaker through the CBU (Completely Built Unit) route. The complete import makes the iconic Beetle brand approachable in India. It is powered by a 1.4 litre turbocharged petrol engine that is mated to a 7 speed automatic gearbox. In addition to the powerful engine option, the Volkswagen Beetle is available only in one variant that has four colour options, Oryx White, Tornado Red, Habanera Orange and Blue Silk. The only variant has a host of safety features such as 6 airbags, ABS, ASR, EDL and EDTC of the Volkswagen Beetle.
